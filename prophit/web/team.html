The Team

Kevin Gilpin, Lead Developer

Kevin supplies the creative and technical energy that has driven the prophIt Profiler from conception to realization. His 7 years of experience at i2 and Trilogy as developer, lead developer, development manager, and architect put him in a position to really understand the problems facing software developers in enterprise software and to propose creative solutions. His experience includes building the software that launched the Automotive Business Unit for Trilogy with a $100M deal with Ford. His personal web site is http://www.ironworks.cc.

During his 7-year tenure at Trilogy he served as a leading technical consultant, deploying some of the most difficult enterprise software implementations and solving the hard problems for his customers.

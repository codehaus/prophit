All About prophIt

prophIt. Every developer who writes code in Java needs a tool like this in their toolbox. prophIt helps make sense out of the performance data you collect on your applications, and helps you and your developers zero in on problem areas. We are convinced that prophIt will save you time in your development cycles, and help you achieve more efficient code. If you found prophIt to be helpful, let us know about it.

Who We Are. We are software engineers who are experts on performance issues in Java applications, and in enterprise software in general. We are familiar with a number of different specialties, from Java, to databases, to J2EE, to CORBA, to XML. We represent decades of experience in enterprise software, software development, and software consulting. We want to help you identify and resolve performance issues with your applications. You can find out more about us at www.westslopesoftware.com/Services/services.html.
